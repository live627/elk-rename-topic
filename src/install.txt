Stop users from double posting and bumping/spamming topics.[list]
[li]Setup this mod at Administration Center » Add-on Settings » Miscellaneous.[/li]
[li]Removes Quick Reply if a bump is not allowed[list]
[li]Does NOT remove the 'Reply' buttons.[/li]
[li]This is intentional and is set up to facilitate users receiving the error message, rather than asking (repeatedly) "Why can't I reply?"[/li]
[/list]
[/li]
[li]The applicable group threshold is the lowest in the user's groups.[list]
[li]Global moderators get 1 hour while newbies have 3 days to wait.[/li]
[li]If a newbie is also a global moderator, then their wait time is 1 hour[/li]
[/list]
[/li]
[/list]
